About Festava 2025

Welcome to Festava Live 2025 — a celebration of rhythm, light, and pure energy! This year, we’re bringing together world-class artists, breathtaking visuals, and thousands of music lovers for three unforgettable nights under the stars.

Feel the Beat

From electrifying EDM drops to soulful acoustic sessions, every stage will vibrate with passion and creativity. Get ready to lose yourself in the sound.

Connect with the Vibe

Festava isn’t just a concert — it’s a community. Meet new friends, discover your next favorite artist, and create memories that’ll last a lifetime.

Beyond the Music

Enjoy immersive art installations, local food stands, and eco-friendly spaces designed for everyone who loves music and the planet. Let’s make 2025 legendary — together.
